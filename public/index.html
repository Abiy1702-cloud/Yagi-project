What We Do

Reliable Transport for Every Need

KG Transport provides professional contracted transportation services across St. Charles and St. Louis Counties, Missouri.

📋 Contract-Based Services Only KG Transport operates exclusively on a contract basis with schools, healthcare facilities, and organizations. We do not provide on-demand, taxi, or rideshare services.
  • 🎒
    Student TransportationSchool routes, after-school programs, extracurricular activities, special education transport
  • 🏥
    Medical / Non-Emergency TransportDoctor visits, dialysis, chemotherapy, physical therapy, hospital discharge
  • ♿
    Wheelchair & Accessible TransportADA-compliant vehicles with ramps and lifts for safe, dignified rides
  • 📅
    Scheduled & Recurring RidesWeekly scheduling, flexible windows, advance booking available

Our Mission

More Than Just a Ride

At KG Transport, we believe professional transportation is built on trust, reliability, and genuine care for the people we serve. Through our contracted partnerships with schools and healthcare organizations, we deliver consistent, dependable service that families and institutions can count on every single day.

We don't take shortcuts. We don't rush. Our drivers show up on time, prepared, and ready to provide whatever level of assistance each passenger requires — from curbside pickup to full door-through-door escort.
